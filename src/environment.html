Biodiversity

Importance of Biodiversity

Biodiversity is the diversity of a ecosystem and it's important for our ecosystem to thrive. Biodiversity helps every facets of our lives including medication production and our livestock

Threats to Biodiversity

  • Habitat destruction
  • Climate change
  • Pollution
  • Overexploitation

Conservation Efforts

  • Protecting habitats
  • Reducing pollution
  • Preventing invasive species
  • Supporting sustainable practices

Human impact on the environment

Humans have a terrible effect on the environment due to pollution, burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and more

It's important to reduce our carbon footprint and protect our environment for future generations
